Outlook

Web Development is a relatively new career, seeing booms in demand recently, especially due to the pandemic. They are projected to witness growth in their demand through the next 20 years. As of now, there are no threats of unemployment for web developers. But after some time, web development may be taken over by robots and AI, posing a threat to the employment of developers.
