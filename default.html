Overview

A web developer is a person who creates and updates websites as a profession. Web Development is a special and important job, which is quite different from others. Web Developers are having an increasing impact on the world. Without websites, our life would be different from the ones we have today. Web Developers have different working conditions, as they principally work from their homes and have less work hours than other careers. Their income is comparable to other major jobs and further growth in demand for developers is expected.
