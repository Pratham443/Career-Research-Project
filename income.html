Income

Web Developers earn based on their experience. Freshers earn $38000 - $50000. The average developer with some experience can earn anywhere from $50000 to $100000. Those with very high experience can have very high incomes such $130000+. Freelancers on the other hand depend on the amount of contracts they get. New freelancers earn only $20000, but experienced and well-known ones can get up to $100000 and more.
