Requirements

To become a web developer, educational requirements are beneficial, but not as necessary. Having a degree in computer science, math, or any other related fields can increase the income of web developers. But other than education, proficiency in programming languages key for web development is a must. Other things that are not required, but are beneficial are mentioned in the Skills page.
